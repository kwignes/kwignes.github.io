About

I'm Kris Wignes, a front-end developer and designer from Vancouver, BC. I studied business at the University of Maine and web development at British Columbia Institute of Technology. I've been in the industry for over 4 years and continue to love every second of it.

I started out as an intern at Vancouver Community Network where I fixed computers and worked on their web-based text messaging platform. This gave me a lot of skills to take the next steps in my career. After the internship, I jumped into various contract jobs, mostly building websites, and also some UI development work.

I've had the opportunity to work with many great clients all over North America in different industries including healthcare, engineering, and tourism.

Some of the services I offer include: website design and development, support and maintenance, and WordPress development and training. I'm most skilled at HTML, CSS, and JavaScript - being the core tools for building a site. I'm also experienced with WordPress, Shopify, PhotoShop, and various other tools and platforms.

Feel free to browse my portfolio and don't hesitate to contact me if you want to work together.
